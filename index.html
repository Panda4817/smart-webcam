🌱 Protect my plants - Smart Webcam

Get alerted when cats 🐈 or birds 🐦 appear in your garden! 🌳

To turn it on, wait for the Tensorflow.js model to load before clicking the button to enable the webcam - at which point it will become visible to use.

To test it out, hold up some pictures of cats or birds, close to your webcam to get a real-time classification! When ready click "Enable Webcam" below and accept access to the webcam when the browser asks.
